Daily passenger numbers in June, 2024

The chart displays the daily passenger count for six metro stations in Pune, India, for June 2024. These stations are: Pimpri Chinchwad Municipal Corporation (PCMC), Vanaz, Pune Municipal Corporation (PMC), Pune Railway, and Ramwadi.
It highlights that PCMC, Pune Railway, Ramwadi, and Vanaz stations each had an average daily ridership exceeding 5,000 passengers. The detailed figures for each station can be found in the chart.

    Additional Insights

  • Overall Growth: Pune Metro has witnessed significant growth in ridership since the inauguration of the Ruby Hall Clinic - Ramwadi route.
  • Station Popularity: PCMC, Pune Railway, Ramwadi, and Vanaz stations consistently saw more than 5,000 passengers per day.
  • Route Preference: Line 2 (Vanaz - Ramwadi) had a higher average daily ridership compared to Line 1 (PCMC - District Court).
  • Revenue Generation: The Metro generated an average daily revenue of Rs. 14,73,648 during June 2024.

Revenue

The chart displays the monthly ridership and revenue statistics for Pune Metro for the year 2023, across two metro lines: Pimpri-Chinchwad to Civil Court (Line 1) and Ruby Hall to Vanaz (Line 2).
It highlights that the total ridership for 2023 reached 93.02 lakh passengers, with Line 1 witnessing 38.13 lakh passengers and Line 2 recording 54.89 lakh passengers. The revenue generated totaled ₹13.96 crore for the year.

    Key Insights

  • Overall Ridership: Pune Metro achieved a total ridership of 93.02 lakh in 2023.
  • Line Comparison: Line 2 (Ruby Hall to Vanaz) had a higher ridership with 54.89 lakh passengers, compared to Line 1's 38.13 lakh.
  • Revenue Distribution: Line 2 generated ₹7.90 crore, whereas Line 1 generated ₹6.05 crore.
  • Peak Month: August 2023 saw the highest ridership with over 20 lakh passengers across both lines, contributing to the highest monthly revenue of ₹3.07 crore.
