Metro Services

Explore the range of services provided by Pune Metro, including online ticketing, the Pune Metro mobile app, and the Vidyarthi Pass for students. Discover how Pune Metro enhances urban travel with its smart card system, accessibility services, and park and ride facilities. Experience reliable and modern transportation at your convenience!

Online Ticketing

Skip the queues and book your metro tickets online. Use Pune Metro’s official website or app to purchase tickets in advance and save time during your commute.

Pune Metro Mobile App

Download the Pune Metro app for real-time information on train schedules, ticketing, and station updates. Stay connected and plan your journey with ease, right from your phone.

Vidyarthi Pass

The Vidyarthi Pass offers students unlimited travel on Pune Metro at a discounted rate. This pass ensures affordable and convenient commuting for students throughout the city!

Smart Card System

Get your Pune Metro Smart Card for hassle-free travel! Recharge it online or at stations, and breeze through entry gates without the need for physical tickets.

Park and Ride Facilities

Pune Metro offers convenient parking facilities at select stations. Park your vehicle and enjoy a seamless ride to your destination without worrying about traffic or parking downtown.

Accessibility Services

Pune Metro stations are equipped with elevators, ramps, and dedicated spaces for differently-abled passengers. Ensuring everyone has access to safe and comfortable metro travel.
