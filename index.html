OUR VISITS

This image captures a bustling Pune Metro station, reflecting the efficiency and modern design of the metro system. Our analysis will delve into the various aspects of Pune Metro, including the number of routes and trains, to understand how these features impact daily operations and passenger experience.
On the Feedbacks page of our Field Project website, we gathered valuable insights from Pune Metro users to analyze their experiences. We created visual charts to display data on peak travel times, passenger demographics, and satisfaction levels. This feedback helps us identify areas for improvement and highlights the efficiency of the Pune Metro system among commuters.
During our visit to one of the Pune Metro stations, we gathered information about the One Pune Card. This contactless smart card allows commuters to travel conveniently across the Pune Metro network. It can be easily recharged online and used for cashless transactions, not just for metro travel but also for various other services in the city, making it a versatile option for daily commuters.
